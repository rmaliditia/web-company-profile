    • The Apple Watch SE is water-resistant up to 50 meters, based on the ISO 22810:2010 standard.
    • It is suitable for activities such as swimming in pools or shallow seawater.
    • It is not recommended for activities like deep diving, water skiing, or using it in saunas.
    • After exposure to saltwater or soapy water, rinse the watch with fresh water.
